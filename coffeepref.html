M36 Coffee Roasters

About:

We are custom coffee roasters who focus on blending and roasting coffee beans for the perfect cup of coffee.
M36 Coffee Roasters believes buying organic fairly traded coffee makes good sense for the environment and for
the farmers who grow the crops. Many coffee beans are grown without fertilizers and pesticides so rewarding
those farmers for their hard work keeps them in business and helps their local economy.

You can find more about M36 Coffee Roasters here

Sweetwaters

About:

Wei and Lisa Bee founded the first Sweetwaters in 1993 in downtown Ann Arbor near their alma mater,
The University of Michigan. Inspired by their love of food and drink and driven by their creativity, Sweetwaters was conceived and brought to life.
The marvelous result: a coffeehouse that celebrates classic café fare alongside globally influenced coffees and teas
in an environment that is both classic and friendly.
